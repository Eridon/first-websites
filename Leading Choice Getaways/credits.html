Security Considerations

Keeping a website safe and secure from potential threats can be an expensive and tedious task. Cyber secruity systems can be difficult to integrate into your own website. This can make it easier for hackers to use your website for malicious activity, almost 1 in every 13 website request is malicious activity, therefore it is important to ensure your website has airtight security.
